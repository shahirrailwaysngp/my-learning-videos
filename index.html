Description

This e-learning portal provides a front-desk view simulation of various railway sections. Recorded with high-precision cameras mounted on locomotives, these videos offer a 1:1 real-world perspective of the tracks.

DISCLAIMER

These videos are for educational/training purposes only. They do not replace actual Road Learning (LR) or physical section monitoring. Always refer to the latest Working Time Table (WTT) and Caution Orders.

Key Features

  • • Real-time understanding of signal locations. (Visual Familiarity: Real-time understanding of signal locations.)
  • • Pre-identification of rising and falling gradient sections. (Gradient Mastery: Pre-identification of steep rising/falling gradients.)
  • • Accurate information about permanent caution zones. (Caution Order Precision: Better spatial awareness of permanent caution zones.)
  • • Helps reduce the risk of Signal Passing At Danger (SPAD). (Safety Enhancement: Reduces the risk of Signal Passing At Danger (SPAD).)
  • • Assists new crew members in faster route learning. (Route Learning: Helps in faster route learning for new crew members.)
  • • Reduces dependency on physical trips for minor route revisions. (Operational Efficiency: Reduces the dependency on physical trips for minor route revisions.)
